Discussion on Driving Deployment with Demand

Kathryn Huff, Scopatz, Flanagan, Bae

University of Illinois

Abstract
I think it could be fruitful to have a discussion about algorithms for driving deployment based on demand (not just of power, but of fuel materials, reprocessing capacity, etc) in each of the existing simulators. This, clearly, is in line with the Demand Driven Cycamore Archetypes NEUP proposal. Anyway, based on the literature review we'll be presenting at global, I'd be happy to give a talk and/or just lead some discussion to have everyone discuss how or whether they handle this challenge in their various simulators.
